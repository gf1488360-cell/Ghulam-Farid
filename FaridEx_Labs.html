User Guide

What is the Lab?

FaridEx Labs helps you check if a product or business idea is practical and financially viable before you build it.

How to use

1. Go to Validation Tests.
2. Select RevenuePilot and click "Run Feasibility Test".
3. Or click "Manual" to read the test specification.

Test Manual

RevenuePilot evaluates monetization structures through a 33-vector calibration array. Inputs: TAM, SAM, SOM, CAC, Regulatory Index. Outputs: Feasibility Growth Score, optimization vectors.
